Importance of culture

Culture is related to the development of our attitude. Our cultural values influence how we approach living. it is the ultimate system of social control where people monitor their own standards and behavior. Our cultural values serve as the founding principles of our life. They shape our thinking, behavior, and personality.


Why citizens should know Rwandan
culture?

Citizens should know Rwandan culture to learn what it means to be a Rwandan mainly by understanding the history of Rwanda from the beginning to prepare a better future. They also learn the Rwandans values such as patriotism, Heroism, Hardworking, Transparency and more to help them develop their beliefs and qualities of a good citizen.
